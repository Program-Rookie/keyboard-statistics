Get Deeper Insights with AI

This application prioritizes your privacy. All core functions run locally, we don't record actual characters, and your raw key data is never uploaded automatically.

To get advanced, personalized reports generated by AI (e.g., pattern recognition, comparisons), follow these steps:

  1. Go to Settings > Data Management.
  2. Use the "Export Data" function to save your keypress data (CSV/JSON) locally.
  3. Visit our external AI analysis service: www.example-ai-analysis-site.com (Note: This is separate. Review its privacy policy).
  4. Voluntarily upload your exported file on that website.
  5. The AI will process your data there and generate a report.

Important: Exporting and uploading data is entirely optional and your responsibility. Understand and agree to the external site's data handling practices before uploading.
